Bait for Utah Lake

Bait depends on the fish so It will be seperated by best bait for fish

Bait for White bass

Worms: Usually under a bobber to keep it off the bottom
Artificial lures: Spinners and jigs do well, the best color is white and chartreuse green.
Live minnows: I have never personally but I hear it's a good technique.


Bait for Channel Catfish

Worms: Not the best choice but always a classic.
Chicken Liver: I only catch Channel Catfish with chicken liver so it's a good choice.
Carp meat: My favorite is carp meat because I catch the largest Channels this way
